## Key Features

### AI Agents
Create multiple AI agents with distinct personalities, languages, and tones. Configure how your agents respond to match your brand voice.

### Knowledge Base
Train your agents using:
- **Files** - PDFs, Word docs, text files
- **Websites** - Crawl and learn from your web pages
- **Q&As** - Define specific question-answer combinations
- **Business Info** - Describe your business in plain text

### Integrations
Connect your AI agent to:
- Embedded web widget
- Facebook Messenger
- Instagram Direct Messages

### Analytics & Insights
Track conversations, monitor performance, and continuously improve your AI agents with built-in analytics.
