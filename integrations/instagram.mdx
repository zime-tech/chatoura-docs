title: "Instagram"

---

Handle Instagram Direct Messages automatically with your AI agent. Connect your Instagram Business or Creator account to provide instant responses.

## Prerequisites

- An Instagram Business or Creator account
- A connected Facebook Page
- Admin access to both accounts
- A Chatoura agent with training data

<Note>
Instagram messaging requires a connected Facebook Page. This is a Meta requirement.
</Note>

## Connecting Instagram

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    Navigate to **Integrations** > **Instagram**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect with Facebook">
    Click **Connect Instagram** (uses Facebook login)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select accounts">
    Choose your Facebook Page and linked Instagram account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant permissions">
    Allow Chatoura to access Instagram messaging
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm connection">
    You'll see a success message when connected
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Required Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `instagram_basic` | Access account information |
| `instagram_manage_messages` | Send and receive DMs |
| `pages_messaging` | Required for Instagram API access |

## Supported Message Types

| Type | Receiving | Sending |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Text messages | ✓ | ✓ |
| Images | ✓ | ✓ |
| Story replies | ✓ | ✓ |
| Story mentions | ✓ | ✓ |
| Quick replies | - | - |

## How It Works

1. User sends a DM or replies to your Story
2. Message is received by Chatoura
3. Your AI agent generates a response
4. Response is sent back as a DM

## Settings

### Response Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Auto-respond | Enable/disable automatic responses |
| Response delay | Add natural delay before responding |
| Handle story replies | Respond to Story replies |
| Handle story mentions | Respond to Story mentions |

## Story Interactions

When someone replies to your Story or mentions you:

- The message includes context about the Story
- AI can reference the Story in its response
- Works the same as regular DM conversations

## Viewing Sessions

All Instagram DMs appear in:

- **Chat History** with Instagram icon badge
- Individual session message threads
- Lead profiles (if lead capture is enabled)

## Escalation

When escalation is triggered:

1. You receive a notification (email/Telegram)
2. AI continues responding unless you pause the session
3. You can respond directly on Instagram DMs
4. Pause the session in Chat History if you want to handle manually

## Disconnecting

1. Go to **Integrations** > **Instagram**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Confirm removal

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't connect Instagram">
    - Ensure you have an Instagram Business or Creator account
    - Verify your Facebook Page is linked to Instagram
    - Check that you're an admin of both accounts
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting your Facebook Page first
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Story replies not working">
    - Verify "Handle story replies" is enabled
    - Check that the Story is still active (not expired)
    - Ensure permissions include story access
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Messages delayed">
    - Instagram API may have slight delays
    - Check your response delay settings
    - Verify credit balance is sufficient
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
