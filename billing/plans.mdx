Chatoura offers flexible plans to match your usage. All plans include core features with differences in limits and capabilities.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Starter | Pro | Business |
|---------|---------|-----|----------|
| AI Agents | 1 | 5 | Unlimited |
| Monthly credits | 1,000 | 10,000 | Custom |
| Knowledge characters | 100,000 | 1,000,000 | Custom |
| Team members | 1 | 5 | Unlimited |
| Integrations | Web only | All | All + Priority |
| Support | Email | Priority | Dedicated |

## Credit System

All plans use a credit-based system:

- **1 credit = 1 AI response**
- Credits refresh monthly
- Unused credits don't roll over
- Add more credits anytime

<Note>
See [Credits](/billing/credits) for details on how credits work.
</Note>

## Starter Plan

Best for individuals and small projects:

- 1 AI agent
- 1,000 credits/month
- 100,000 character knowledge base
- Web widget integration
- Email support

## Pro Plan

Best for growing businesses:

- Up to 5 AI agents
- 10,000 credits/month
- 1,000,000 character knowledge base
- All integrations (Web, Facebook, Instagram)
- Priority email support
- Team collaboration (5 members)

## Business Plan

Best for enterprises:

- Unlimited AI agents
- Custom credit volume
- Custom knowledge limits
- All integrations with priority access
- Dedicated support
- Unlimited team members
- Custom features available

## Changing Plans

### Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select new plan
4. Complete payment

Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged the prorated difference.

### Downgrading

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm change

Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

<Warning>
Downgrading may reduce your limits. Ensure your usage fits the new plan.
</Warning>

## Billing Cycle

- Plans are billed monthly
- Payment is due at the start of each cycle
- Cancel anytime before renewal
- No long-term contracts required

## Payment Methods

Accepted payment methods:

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal (where available)

## Invoices

Access your invoices:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Billing History**
3. Download invoices as PDF
