Chatoura offers flexible plans to match your usage. All plans include core features with differences in limits and capabilities.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Free | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|------|---------|-----|------------|
| Price | $0/month | $30/month | $110/month | Custom |
| Monthly credits | 50 | 1,500 | 6,000 | Custom |
| AI Agents | 1 | 1 | 3 | Custom |
| Knowledge documents | 1 | 3 | 10 | Custom |
| Team members | 1 | 1 | 4 | Unlimited |

## Credit System

All plans use a credit-based system:

- **1 credit = 1 AI response**
- Credits refresh monthly
- Unused credits don't roll over
- Contact sales for additional credits

<Note>
See [Credits](/billing/credits) for details on how credits work.
</Note>

## Plan Tiers

### Free Plan

Get started with AI chatbots:

- **50 credits** per month
- 1 chatbot
- 1 knowledge document
- 1 team member

### Starter Plan

Perfect for small teams and startups:

- **1,500 credits** per month
- 1 chatbot
- 3 knowledge documents
- 1 team member

### Pro Plan

For growing businesses with advanced needs:

- **6,000 credits** per month
- 3 chatbots
- 10 knowledge documents
- 4 team members
- All integrations (Web, Facebook, Instagram)

### Enterprise Plan

Custom solutions for large organizations:

- Everything in Pro
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority support
- SLA guarantee
- Custom analytics

## Changing Plans

### Upgrading

1. Go to **Subscription** page
2. Select the plan you want to upgrade to
3. Click **Upgrade**
4. Complete payment

Upgrades take effect immediately.

### Downgrading

1. Go to **Subscription** page
2. Select a lower tier plan
3. Confirm the downgrade

Downgrades are scheduled and take effect at the next billing cycle.

<Warning>
Downgrading may reduce your limits. Ensure your usage fits the new plan.
</Warning>

## Billing Cycle

- Plans are billed monthly
- Payment is due at the start of each cycle
- Cancel anytime before renewal
- No long-term contracts required

## Payment Methods

Accepted payment methods:

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
